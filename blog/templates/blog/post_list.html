Random Dungeon Generator Game!

This game is reminiscent of the old text-based adventure games that used to come out, especially about D&D which was incredibly popular to nerds at the time. This game randomly generates a dungeon for you, with 10 encounter-able monsters and loot galore. You play as a level 5 fighter with an HP range of 35 to 50 points with an attack modifier of 3 and an armor class of 18. You'll face off with goblins, skeletons, and even a gelatinous cube! Along the way you can find armor and even a new weapon that will increase your attack and defense modifiers. Head over to the GitHub for installation instructions.
